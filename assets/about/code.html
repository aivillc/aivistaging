Our Story

Founded by veterans from Amazon, Apple, and Coca-Cola, our journey began with a simple observation: businesses were losing customers at an unprecedented revenue due to slow response times and inefficient customer engagement.

We knew AI could solve this, but existing solutions were complex, expensive to implement, or simply didn't deliver. We envisioned a world where powerful AI-driven communication was not just for large corporations, but simple enough for any business to deploy.

Today, we're proud to help businesses of all sizes provide instant, personalized experiences, eliminate dominant leads, and drive measurable growth through AI-powered automation.
