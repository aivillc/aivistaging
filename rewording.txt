## CONTENT STRATEGY RECOMMENDATIONS

### 1. HEADLINE FORMULA
Replace generic headlines with benefit-driven alternatives using this framework:

**OLD FORMULA:** [Product Feature] + [Generic Descriptor]

### F.1: MESSAGING CONSISTENCY FRAMEWORK

**Problem:** Same benefit stated differently across pages creates confusion.

**Solution:** Standardize core messages:

**VALUE PROP (use consistently):**
"AIVI converts leads 391% faster by answering every call, qualifying prospects
instantly, and booking appointments automaticallyâ€”24/7, without adding headcount."

**When to use full version:** Homepage hero, About page, sales decks
**When to use short version:** "Convert leads 391% faster with AI voice agents"

---

**METRIC USAGE STANDARDS:**

CORRECT:
- "391% faster lead conversion"
- "Leads convert 3.91x faster with AIVI"

INCORRECT:
- "391% increase" (ambiguous: increase in what?)
- "Up to 391%" (weakens credibility)
- "391% ROI" (different metric entirely)

**Rule:** Pick ONE way to state each metric across all pages.

### F.2: CTA OPTIMIZATION FRAMEWORK

**Problem:** Inconsistent CTAs confuse users about next steps.

**Solution:** Standardize CTA hierarchy:

**PRIMARY CTA (all pages):** "Book a Demo" or "Start Free Trial"
**SECONDARY CTA (all pages):** "View Pricing" or "See Case Studies"

**ELIMINATE THESE CTAs:**
- "Learn More" (too vague)
- "Discover" (too passive)
- "Explore" (no clear outcome)
- "Contact Us" (too generic)

**CTA COPYWRITING FORMULA:**
[Action Verb] + [Clear Outcome] + [Barrier Removal]

Examples:
- "Book a Demo â€“ See AIVI Live" (action + outcome)
- "Start Free Trial â€“ No Credit Card" (action + barrier removal)
- "Calculate Your Savings â€“ Free ROI Tool" (action + outcome + value)

### F.3: FAQ STRATEGY & SEO OPTIMIZATION

**Problem:** FAQs are randomly ordered, miss SEO opportunities.

**Solution:** Structure FAQs by customer journey stage:

**AWARENESS STAGE FAQS** (What is AIVI? Who is it for?)

- "What industries use AIVI?"

**CONSIDERATION STAGE FAQS** (Can it work for us? Technical fit?)
- "What CRMs and phone systems does AIVI integrate with?"

- "Can AIVI handle [industry-specific scenario]?"

**DECISION STAGE FAQS** (Pricing, onboarding, risk)
- "What's included in the free trial?"
- "What happens if I exceed my lead limit?"
- "Can I cancel anytime?"

**SEO OPTIMIZATION:**
- Use long-tail keywords in questions ("How much does AI voice automation cost for law firms?")
- Include year in answers for freshness ("As of 2025, AIVI integrates with...")
- Add "People Also Ask" section with related questions
